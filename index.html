Who is Baldwin IV?

The Leper King of Jerusalem

Baldwin IV, known as the Leper King, was the ruler of Jerusalem from 1174 to 1185. Despite being afflicted with leprosy, he led his kingdom with remarkable courage and strategic acumen, achieving notable victories during his reign.
