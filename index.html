Toilet Repair

We're IPS—you're local toilet fixers

When your toilet isn't working correctly, it's no laughing matter. Aside from the unsanitary conditions caused by a clogged or leaking toilet, you also risk a more severe leak from the plumbing lines connected to your toilet if the toilet is loose or unstable.

Without proper maintaince, a leaking or faulty toilet can lead to significant water damage to your property. If you need information, or help resolving common toilet issues, or if you need any of the toilet repair services listed on this page, don't hesitate to contact us.

Toilet Replacement & Installation

Upgrade and Save

If your toilet has developed cracks or leaks, uses far too much water per flush, or is simply a shade of green not seen in 20 years, it may be time to replace it.

With so many options on the market, choosing a toilet to fit your living space or office can be challenging. Regardless of which type of toilet you choose, one thing remains: Installing a new toilet without the proper knowledge can be a dirty and tedious process.

We recommend hiring certified plumbers and professional technicians for new toilet installation or toilet replacement. Feel free to contact us, its the quickest way to get your new toilet installed and working properly.
